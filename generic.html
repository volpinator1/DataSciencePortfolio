About me

Hello, my name is Evan Volpini, and I am a current undergraduate student at the University of Waterloo, pursuing a degree in mathematics. With a strong passion for data and analysis, I have been developing my skills in data science and statistics to solve problems and extract insights from data.

Please feel free to reach out to me if you have any questions or would like to connect. Thank you for visiting!
